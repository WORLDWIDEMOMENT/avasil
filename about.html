This is not a portfolio.
This is an evolving system — built at the intersection of spatial design, research, AI experimentation and visual storytelling.

Professional precision. Underground sensitivity.

I believe a space isn't truly complete until it's felt. Growing up within Slavic visual culture, I was driven to understand how environment shapes experience — which led me across Southern Europe, its light, textures, and material memory.

My practice moves between architecture, interiors, master planning and brand strategy. But architecture was never my only language — video, collage, custom clothing and photography aren't separate hobbies. They are how I explore the atmospheres spaces hold.

I don't separate designing a building from crafting an image. Whether it's a tropical villa, a master plan or a film — the goal is the same: work that resonates, imprints, connects people to their environment.
