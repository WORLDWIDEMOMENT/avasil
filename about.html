Biography

My work lives at the intersection of architecture, art and narrative — a search for the emotional core of the places we inhabit. Trained in Warsaw, sharpened in Lisbon, Cartagena and Marbella — living across contexts taught me that powerful design isn't imposed, it's discovered through deep observation.

I navigate from concept to construction, collaborating across architecture, interiors, master planning and brand strategy. But architecture was never my only language — video, collage, custom clothing and photography are how I explore the atmospheres spaces hold.

This is not a portfolio.
This is an evolving architectural system —
built at the intersection of spatial design, urban research, AI experimentation
and visual storytelling.

Professional precision. Underground sensitivity.
